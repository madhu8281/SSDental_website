Dr. Shahul Hameed Sattar is a highly qualified dental professional specializing in Prosthodontics and Implantology with extensive training from prestigious institutions worldwide. He is the Clinical Director of Dr. SS Dental Care & Advanced Dental Implant Center in Bangalore.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience, Dr. Shahul has earned a reputation for delivering exceptional results in cosmetic dentistry, full-mouth rehabilitation, and complex implant surgeries. His patient-first approach and meticulous attention to detail have made him one of the most sought-after implantologists in Bangalore.

He has trained under world-renowned dental surgeons and regularly participates in international conferences to stay at the forefront of dental innovation. Dr. Shahul is known not just for his skill, but also for his ability to put anxious patients at ease.

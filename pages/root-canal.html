What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root canal treatment (RCT) removes infected pulp inside a tooth, cleans and seals the root canal to prevent further infection, saving the natural tooth.

When Do You Need a Root Canal?

  • Severe toothache or prolonged sensitivity to heat/cold
  • Darkening or discolouration of the tooth
  • Swelling or tenderness in nearby gums
  • A persistent pimple on the gums
  • Cracked or chipped tooth exposing the pulp

Our Approach - Comfortable and Modern

We use the latest rotary endodontic equipment under local anesthesia. Most single-canal cases are completed in a single visit with minimal discomfort.

After Treatment

Mild soreness for a few days is normal. The tooth is then restored with a crown. With proper care, a root-canal-treated tooth can last a lifetime.

What is Orthodontic Treatment?

Orthodontics corrects misaligned teeth and jaws. Straight teeth are easier to clean, look better, and function more effectively — improving both oral health and confidence.

Our Orthodontic Services

  • Traditional metal braces - reliable, effective, affordable
  • Ceramic braces - tooth-colored for a subtler look
  • Clear aligners - removable, nearly invisible trays
  • Lingual braces - fitted behind the teeth
  • Retainers for post-treatment maintenance
  • Treatment for deep bite, open bite, spacing, and crowding

Clear Aligners

Custom transparent trays that gradually shift teeth. Removable, making eating and brushing easy. Treatment typically takes 6-18 months.

Who Can Benefit?

Both children and adults benefit. Ideal age is 10-14 years, but adults can be successfully treated at any age.
